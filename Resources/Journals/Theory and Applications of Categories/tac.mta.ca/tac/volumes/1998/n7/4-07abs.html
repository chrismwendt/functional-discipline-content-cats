Applications of Peiffer pairings in the Moore complex of a simplicial group

A. Mutlu, T. Porter

Generalising a result of Brown and Loday, we give for n=3 and 4, a decomposition of the group, d_nNG_n, of boundaries of a simplicial group G as a product of commutator subgroups. Partial results are given for higher dimensions. Applications to 2-crossed modules and quadratic modules are discussed.
