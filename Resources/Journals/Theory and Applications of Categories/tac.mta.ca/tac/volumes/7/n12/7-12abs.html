Quasi-varieties of presheaves

Enrico M. Vitale

In analogy with the varietal case, we give an abstract characterization of those categories occurring as regular epireflective subcategories of presheaf categories such that the inclusion functor preserves small sums.

Keywords:

2000 MSC: 18A40, 18F20.

Theory and Applications of Categories, Vol. 7, 2000, No. 12, pp 236-238.
