Finiteness of a Non-abelian Tensor Product of Groups

Nick Inassaridze

Some sufficient conditions for finiteness of a generalized non-abelian tensor product of groups are established extending Ellis' result for compatible actions.

Keywords: Non­abelian tensor product of groups,Comp­subgroup,Comp­pairs, compatibility resolution,half compatible actions.

AMS Classification (1991): 18G50.

Theory and Applications of Categories, Vol. 2, 1996, No. 5, pp 54-61.
