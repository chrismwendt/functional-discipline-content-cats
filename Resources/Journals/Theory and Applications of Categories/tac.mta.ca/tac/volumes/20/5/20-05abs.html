Coherence for pseudodistributive laws revisited

F. Marmolejo and R. J. Wood

In this paper we show that eight coherence conditions suffice for the definition of a pseudodistributive law between pseudomonads.

Keywords: pseudomonads, pseudodistributive laws, coherence

2000 MSC: 18C15,18D05

Theory and Applications of Categories, Vol. 20, 2008, No. 5, pp 74-84.
