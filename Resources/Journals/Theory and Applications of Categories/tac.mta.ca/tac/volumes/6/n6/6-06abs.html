Comparing coequalizer and exact completions

M. C. Pedicchio and J. Rosicky

We characterize when the coequalizer and the exact completion of a category $\cal C$ with finite sums and weak finite limits coincide.

Keywords: exact completion, coequalizer completion, variety.

1991 MSC: 18A99.

Theory and Applications of Categories, Vol. 6, 1999, No. 6, pp 77-82.
