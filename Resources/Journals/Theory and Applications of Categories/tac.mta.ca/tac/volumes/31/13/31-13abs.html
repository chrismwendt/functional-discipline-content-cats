A cotriple construction of a simplicial algebra used in the definition of higher Chow groups

Jason Polak

We present a brief and simple cotriple description of the simplicial algebra used in Bloch's construction of the higher Chow groups.

Keywords: cotriple, simplicial algebra

2010 MSC: 18C15
