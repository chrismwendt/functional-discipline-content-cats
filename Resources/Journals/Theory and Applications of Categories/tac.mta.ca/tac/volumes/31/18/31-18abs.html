The morphism axiom for n-angulated categories

Emilie Arentz-Hansen, Petter Andreas Bergh and Marius Thaule

We show that the morphism axiom for n-angulated categories is redundant.

Keywords: Triangulated categories, n-angulated categories, morphism axiom
