The local Joyal model structure

Nicholas J. Meadows

The Joyal model structure on simplicial sets is extended to a model structure on the simplicial presheaves on a small site, in which the cofibrations are monomorphisms and the weak equivalences are local (or stalkwise) Joyal equivalences. The model structure is shown to be left proper.

Keywords: simplicial presheaves, quasi-categories, model structure duality, trace, derivator, absolute colimit

2010 MSC: 18G30, 18F20, 55U35
