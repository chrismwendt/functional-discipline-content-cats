Stacks and sheaves of categories as fibrant objects, II

Alexandru E. Stanculescu

We revisit what we call the fibred topology on a fibred category over a site and we prove a few basic results concerning this topology. We give a general result concerning the invariance of a 2-category of stacks under change of base.

Keywords: Grothendieck topology, fibred category, stack, model category

2010 MSC: 18F10, 18D30, 18G55

Theory and Applications of Categories, Vol. 31, 2016, No. 11, pp 330-3364.
