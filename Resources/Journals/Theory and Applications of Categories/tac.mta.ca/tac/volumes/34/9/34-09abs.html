Topoi of parametrized objects

Marc Hoyois

We give necessary and sufficient conditions on a presentable $\infty$-category $C$ so that families of objects of $C$ form an $\infty$-topos. In particular, we prove a conjecture of Joyal that this is the case whenever $C$ is stable.

Keywords: Higher topos theory
