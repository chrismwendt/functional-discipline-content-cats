A note on the categorical congruence distributivity

Dominique Bourn

Having given a characterization of the categorical congruence modularity getting rid of the assumption that the ground category is regular, we give now a characterization of the categorical congruence distributivity. We have a look as well at the case where the congruence distributivity is only involved, in some sense, for a subclass $\Gamma$ of equivalence relations.

Keywords: Suprema of equivalence relations, congruence modularity, congruence distributivity

2010 MSC: 08B10, 08C05, 18A20, 18A32
