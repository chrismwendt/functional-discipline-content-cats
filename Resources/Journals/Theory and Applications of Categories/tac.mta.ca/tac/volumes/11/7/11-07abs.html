Resolutions by Polygraphs

François Métayer

A notion of resolution for higher-dimensional categories is defined, by using polygraphs, and basic invariance theorems are proved.

Keywords: $n$-category, polygraph, resolution, homotopy, homology

2000 MSC: 18D05

Theory and Applications of Categories , Vol. 11, 2003, No. 7, pp 148-184.
